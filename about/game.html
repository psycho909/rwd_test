Game background

A legend goes that there lived a demon in a Bratislava castle. Being under its curse, all crops on the earth withered for the lack of water. To break the spell, five spirits were dispatched to the secular world by the deity. Confronted with such a mighty demon, they had no other choice but to sacrifice themselves and transformed into five spars to finally defeat the demon, namely, Pupil of the Earth, Blue Water, Dancing Wind, Flame Spirit and Angel’s Tear. Afterwards, the place was honored as “Holy Land”, and the five spars was praised as “Fortune Spars” for they broke the spell and brought good fortune.
