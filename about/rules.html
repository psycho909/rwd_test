Rules

  • 1. How to play
    There are five spots in each ticket. Please pick 5 numbers from the field of 0 through 9. Each play only costs one euro. Multiple bets are also accepted on a single Fortune Spars draw.
  • 2. Cost
    Each Fortune Spars is only 1 euro to play.
  • 3. When to play
    Summer time (April 25-October 25)
    LT: 06:11 am-09:01pm
    Standard time (October 25- April 25)
    LT: 05:11 am- 08:01 pm
    Note: Drawing will not take place at other times. It takes place every ten minutes.
  • 4. How to buy tickets
    Retailer locations can be found in various major cities. Players can simply go to any of them to buy a paper ticket. Each ticket includes Date of Draw, Stage, number spots and a unique anti-counterfeiting code. Please take good care of your ticket. No partly soiled or damaged tickets will be accepted, and they shall be considered invalid. Prizes can only be claimed when winning tickets are presented.
