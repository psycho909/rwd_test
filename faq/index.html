  • Q:Where to claim winning prizes?
    A:Prizes of €500 or less can be claimed at any Fortune Spars retailer, regardless of where you purchased your ticket. For prizes of €501 or more, please contact us by phone calls, or go to Tipos.
  • Q:When are Fortune Spars draws held?
    A:Drawings are held every ten minutes daily from 06:01 am to 09:01 pm.
  • Q:How many Fortune Spars drawings in total each day?
    A:There are 90 draws per day.
  • Q:What if I lose my ticket?
    A:Prizes can only be claimed when winning tickets are presented. You are responsible for keeping your ticket safe.
  • Q:How much does each ticket cost?
    A:The cost of each play is only one euro.
  • Q:Where to buy Fortune Spars tickets?
    A:tickets can be purchased in any Fortune Spars retailer.
